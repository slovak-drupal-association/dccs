Where?

The Faculty of Mathematics, Physics and Informatics is one of 13 faculties of the Comenius University in Bratislava. The faculty provides higher education in mathematics, physics and informatics, as well as teacher training in subjects related to these branches of study. It was established in 1980 by separating from the Faculty of Natural Sciences under the name of Faculty of Mathematics and Physics. Its name was changed to the contemporary name in 2000.

In 2015, Faculty of Mathematics, Physics and Informatics was ranked first in the group of natural sciences in the ranking of faculties in Slovakia by the Academic Ranking and Rating Agency.

The faculty is located in locality called Mlynská dolina. This locality hosts multiple universities and even campus.

How to get there?

From railway station Bratislava hlavna stanica:
  • take bus no. 32 direction "Dlhe Diely" and get off on stop "Botanicka zahrada"
From railway station Bratislava Petrzalka:
  • take bus no. 80 direction "Kolarovo namestie", get off on stop "Zochova", take tram 5/9 direction "Dubravka"/"Karlova Ves" and get off on stop "Botanicka zahrada"
  • take bus no. 91 direction "Novy most", get off on stop "Novy most", take tram 4 direction "Dubravka" and get off on stop "Botanicka zahrada"
From bus station:
  • take bus no. 70 direction "Novy most", get off on stop "Novy most", take tram 4 direction "Dubravka"/"Karlova Ves" and get off on stop "Botanicka zahrada"
From airport:
  • take bus no. 61 direction "Hlavna stanica", get off on stop "Hlavna stanica" and follow steps directions in section "From railway station Bratislava hlavna stanica"

You need to buy ticket for public transportation in advance from ticket machines. Basic ticket for 15 minutes ride costs 0.70€. Ticket for 1h ride costs 1.30€. Tickets must be validated right after you enter vehicle. You need additional ticket for your luggage.
