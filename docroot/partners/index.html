While we are prudent with our organizational plans and the related financial planning, we can only cover the general conference expenses with the help of our sponsors.

Sponsoring the event is not only a great way to promote your brand as a company or organization, but gives a lot to the Drupal community by providing the financial background to make this event happen in a professional way.
