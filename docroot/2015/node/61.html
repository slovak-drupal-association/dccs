DrupalCampCS
Three days of Drupal action!

One month left until DrupalCampCS

3 days of sprints

Drupal 8 is almost here but there is still a lot of bugs (we saw that, this page is on Drupal8). Come and help solve many of this issues. You have never worked with Drupal 8 or you have never contributed to community? No problem! We are preparing sprint for complete begginers with well-skilled mentors.

Sessions

On Saturday there will be 12 sessions in two rooms. Some of Drupal community rockstars are comming. We are going to reveal them in next days. We tried to cover each of topics, that some of you filled in our survey.

Do you have interesting topic, which you want to talk about? Write us! This is ideal place, where you can try presenting in front of huge crowd of people.

Trainings

This is first time, when professional trainers are coming to our camp. We prepared 1 Drupal training (topic will be specified later). As we have good relations with Sympfony creators, they are sending us from SensioLabs UK trainer for Symfony2 framework.
