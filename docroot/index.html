About DrupalCampCS

Two days of Drupal action!

DrupalCamp CS is 2-day event for fans and professionals of Drupal and web technologies. We are bringing 2 days of sprints, 1 day of sessions and 1 day of trainings.

DrupalCampCS is a moving event of Czech ans Slovak Drupal communities. This year is being held in Bratislava, Slovakia and takes place on 28-29 May 2016. DrupalCampCS is the event for everyone interested in Drupal.

For individuals, it is a greate place to learn something. For companies, it offers chance to network and explore new business opportunities.
